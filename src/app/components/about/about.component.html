Our Story

Founded in 2010, Naveen & Nikilesh Developers began with a focused vision: land plotting and development. What started as a specialized team dedicated to transforming raw land into organized, well-planned layouts has evolved into a comprehensive construction and development powerhouse.

Over the years, we've successfully completed hundreds of land development projects, creating residential plots and commercial spaces that have shaped communities. Our expertise in land surveying, plot layout, infrastructure planning, and regulatory compliance has earned us the trust of clients across the region.

Today, we're expanding our wings beyond land development into full-scale construction, including high-rise buildings and skyscrapers. Embracing modern construction techniques and sustainable practices, we're ready to take on projects of any scale while maintaining the core values that have defined us from the beginning: integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction.
